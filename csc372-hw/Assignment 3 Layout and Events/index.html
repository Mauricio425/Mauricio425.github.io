Welcome!

Welcome! to Kung fu-d Review! If you love discovering delicious eats, you’re in the right place. Whether you’re craving a comforting plate of Arroz con Pollo from San Luis Restaurant, a sweet and crispy Cosmic Waffle from Astro Dogs, or a perfectly brewed Cortado from Coffeeology, this blog has got you covered. I’ll be sharing my thoughts on three amazing spots, each offering a mix of savory, sweet, and satisfying flavors. So, if you're ready to explore some must-try dishes, let’s dive into the world of great food!

Restaurants

San Luis Restaurant

Located at 1503 W Gate City Blvd in Greensboro, NC, San Luis Restaurant is a go-to spot for authentic Mexican cuisine and delightful desserts. Whether you're craving a hearty plate of Arroz con Pollo, a crispy and indulgent Fried Ice Cream, or a classic Flan, this restaurant delivers bold flavors and satisfying portions. Open seven days a week from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, it's the perfect place for a casual lunch or a flavorful dinner. Although they don’t currently offer promotions, the delicious food speaks for itself!

Recommended Dishes

Astro Dogs

Located at 1621 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC, Astro Dogs serves up delicious Korean-style corndogs with crispy coatings and gooey fillings. Alongside their signature corndogs, they offer tasty sides like Street Corn and warm Hot Chocolate. Open late most nights (until midnight Thurs-Sat), it’s the perfect spot for a fun and flavorful snack!

Coffeeoligy

Located at 423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403, Coffeeoligy specializes in coffee, breakfast items, and pastries. Open daily—Monday through Friday from 7 AM–6 PM and Saturday–Sunday from 9 AM–4 PM.
